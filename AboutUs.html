Learn new skills and boost your learning with us

UniCourse is an online learning academy

It provides online learning to equip students with academic skills, and enable them to have high impact in job sectors, private, and teaching sectors by making meaningful online learning experiences available from anywhere, at any time. UniCourse courses cover a wide range of topics, including C, C++, Java, Html, Swift and also development, leadership, and much more. Our curriculum is designed in consultation with industry experts to provide the most relevant and up-to-date material, and certificates are issued on course completion. UniCourse courses are designed to not only increase our students' employability, but also to challenge them to think about their values, purpose and the impact they can have in their lives. UniCourse is building on UniCourse's mission to empower and engage students to be active and make meaningful impact engineering sectors. By making our content available online, we hope to extend our own reach and impact in building a prosperous, just, and inclusive world driven by courageous, compassionate, and competent people.
